# 🎵 What Makes a Song Popular?

An exploratory data analysis (EDA) project that investigates whether a song's audio characteristics influence its popularity on Spotify.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to explore the relationship between Spotify song popularity and various audio features such as danceability, energy, loudness, acousticness, instrumentalness, tempo, genre, and explicit content.

---

## 📊 Dataset

- **Dataset Size:** 113,999 songs
- **Features:** 20
- **Source:** Spotify Songs Dataset

---

## 🔍 Questions Explored

- Which artists appear most frequently?
- Which genres have the highest average popularity?
- How is song popularity distributed?
- Does energy affect popularity?
- Does danceability affect popularity?
- Are explicit songs more popular?
- Which audio features are most correlated with popularity?

---

## 📈 Key Findings

- Most songs have low to moderate popularity.
- Approximately **14%** of songs have a popularity score of **0**.
- Only **2 songs** have a popularity score of **100**.
- Loudness has the strongest positive correlation with popularity, but the relationship is weak.
- Instrumentalness has the strongest negative correlation.
- No single audio feature strongly predicts song popularity.
